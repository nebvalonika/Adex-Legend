~About Game~

Adex Legends is an online multiplayer battle royale game featuring squads of three players using pre-made characters with distinctive abilities, called "Legends", similar to those of hero shooters. Alternate modes have been introduced allowing for single and for two-player squads since the game's release.

Shop
The in-game shop of Adex Legends is called the Rotating Shop, which lets you unlock specific cosmetics and characters using Adex Coins, its premium currency, or Legend Tokens, which players can earn through gameplay. Items in the Rotating Shop change periodically.
